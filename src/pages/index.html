Step 1 – The Brief

You don't need a new site. What you need is a sales and marketing funnel that allows you to expand your business online. The benefits of a strong web presence is a) 24/7 availability b) trust building c) educating your customers and d) making sure they can reach out at the exact right time in the sale.

If done wrong, the above is useless. Today's customers are savvy and they can spot a fake, a $29 WordPress template, or just get plain frustrated and leave... never to return. Your job should be to capture the interest of your silent customers and drive them in a direction that has a direct and concrete benefit to your business.

A 90-minute interview followed up by a 3-page brief, clearly identifies core strengths, opportunities, and a strategic plan to move your web presence forward.
